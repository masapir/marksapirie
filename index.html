Mark Sapirie

Mark teaches mathematics, physics, and Latin in Jupiter, Florida. Prior to teaching, Mark worked with the intellectual property and business litigation practices of several international law firms in Washington, DC and Miami. He graduated from William & Mary Law School in the top 5% of his class and was awarded Order of the Coif.

Mark has an S.B. in Mathematics with Computer Science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He earned the Swiss Maturité Fédérale Scientifique while studying at the Collège Champittet in Lausanne, Switzerland, is fluent in French, and has a basic working understanding of German.
